The idea

A Minecraft world that behaves more like a real one.

01

Realistic survival

Gameplay is planned around climate, health, resources, money, work, transport, and risk instead of simple minigame loops.

02

Living society

Players should be able to build towns, use services, take jobs, travel, trade, and shape the world over time.

03

Earth-scale planning

BlueMap, land borders, regions, and real-world geography are part of the foundation for future gameplay systems.
